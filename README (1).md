- Videos auto-play when you navigate to their slide

### Troubleshooting Videos

If videos don't play automatically:

1. **Check Google Drive Sharing Settings**:
   - Files must be set to "Anyone with the link" can view
   - Ensure viewer permissions are enabled

2. **Browser Autoplay Restrictions**:
   - Some browsers block autoplay with sound
   - Click the video to start playback manually
   - Consider muting videos for reliable autoplay

3. **Bandwidth Limits**:
   - Google Drive has daily bandwidth quotas
   - Large files may be throttled
   - Consider uploading to YouTube (unlisted) as an alternative
